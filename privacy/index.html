PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Ultimate Security Solutions LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Ultimate Security Suite mobile application and the website www.usstech.net. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and display name for authentication purposes only. We do not access your Google Drive, calendar, or other Google services.

Project Data: Blueprints, site plans, device placements, and cable routing data you create within the Ultimate Security Suite app are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

Location Data: The app uses your device's location services only when you explicitly grant permission, solely for the purpose of satellite map targeting and site survey features. Location data is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Camera and Photos: The app requests camera and photo library access only when you use the Live Site Survey feature to capture on-site photos. These images are processed temporarily for AI analysis and are not stored on our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your account via Google Sign-In
  • Provide AI-powered blueprint analysis and site intelligence features through Google's Gemini API
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Respond to support inquiries

4. Third-Party Services

Google OAuth: We use Google Sign-In for authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to data collected by Google during the sign-in process.

Google Gemini AI: Blueprint analysis and site intelligence features use Google's Gemini API. Images and prompts you submit are processed by Google in accordance with Google's AI privacy practices. We do not store the AI responses on our own servers.

Esri Map Services: Satellite imagery is provided by Esri World Imagery. Map tile requests are subject to Esri's privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

Project data is stored locally on your device. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data transmitted between the application and our service endpoints. All API communications use HTTPS encryption.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated usage data for analytics purposes only.

7. Data Retention

Account information is retained for the duration of your account. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Revoke Google OAuth access at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Uninstall the application to remove all locally stored project data

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes through the application or by email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
