COMMON QUESTIONS

What insurance do you carry?
We carry $2M General Liability and full Workers' Compensation coverage. We'll provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) naming your company as additionally insured upon request.
Can you work under our brand?
Yes. We operate as a white-label extension of your team — wearing your branded PPE, using your documentation templates, and representing your company on-site.
What's your service area?
Primary coverage is the Kansas City metro area within a 100-mile radius. We travel for multi-site rollouts — contact us for project-based coverage outside KC.
How quickly can you dispatch?
For established partners, same-day dispatch is available for urgent requests. Standard scheduling is typically 24-48 hours. We also support after-hours and weekend deployments.
Do you provide your own tools and equipment?
Yes. Our vans are fully stocked with ladders, cable testers, termination tools, power tools, and consumables. We arrive job-ready.
